5 Day Motorcycle Tour: Montenegro

Enjoy a 5 day motorcycle ride with experienced tour guides around wild natural beauty of Montenegro.

Day 1:

We reserve our first day for the most demanding part of the journey, riding narrow roads with poor asphalt, but with the most beautiful scenery and nature. We leave from Podgorica via Rijeka Crnojevici, following the old road to Bar, St. Stefan and further down the Adriatic arterial highway all the way to Morinj.

Day 2:

The ride starts in the surreal Boka Bay, following the road over Lovcen toward Kolasin, climbing from the zero to over 1000 m altitude. We will spend the second night in Berane, on a local family farm where we will enjoy home cooked meals and taste the food that leaves no palate indifferent.

Day 3:

With a heavy heart but full bellies, we continue our journey towards Alipasa Springs, the biggest karst spring in Montenegro. We leave Prokletije National Park to return to Podgorica through the amazing Cijevna Canyon. We will visit vine cellar Šipčanik, located in a former Yugoslavia underground airspace, reaching our overnight stay overwhelmed with impressions.

Day 4:

Wake up in Tunjevo, to chirping birds and warm coffee. After breakfast we continue over Glava Zete (where car races are held, to shake things up) climbing to Ostrog Monastery, the most sacred pilgrimage in Montenegro and the most visited Christian orthodox monastery in Europe. From there, we head to Piva Lake, the heart of Durmitor National Park. We will be making a short coffee break to rest before we embark on another adventure – the Mt. Durmitor Ring. It is one of the three points you need to visit to truly experience the most beautiful places in the Balkans! Afterward, we will arrive to Zabljak to savour our impressions.

Day 5:

We leave Zabljak to head to Djurdjevica Tara Bridge while passing through Crna Poda virgin forest, with some of the oldest and the tallest pine trees in Europe, to reach another NP Biogradska Gora with Biogradsko Lake in the heart of it. After that, we will be following winding roads along Moraca River Canyon to Podgorica, to wrap up the tour and start dreaming of new adventures.

A five day tour cost: 1.250,00€ / person.

3 Day Motorcycle Tour: Montenegro

Day 1:

From Podgorica, we ride through Rijeka Crnojevici, Virpazar, following a road leading through a chestnut forest, along the edges of Skadar Lake. We continue all the way to Shasko Lake, through the Old Town of Bar, St. Stefan and further down the Adriatic coastal highway to Morinj.

Day 2:

The ride starts from the mesmerising Boka Kotorska Bay, over Mt. Lovcen to Kolasin, climbing from zero to 1000 m in altitude. We will spend the second night in Berane, on a local family farm where we will enjoy home cooked meals and taste the food that leaves no palate indifferent.

Day 3:

The best highlights are reserver for the last day. We will be riding through Crna Poda virgin forest to Zabljak and Sedlo pass at 1907 m altitude, the highest pass in Montenegro, to continue through the unparalleled Durmitor National Park and descend to Piva Lake, to enjoy stunning views. We press on along the scenic road and stacked curves to Niksic returning to Podgorica into the finale of this adventure.
